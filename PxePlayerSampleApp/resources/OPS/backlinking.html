Backlinking

This page has href links on it to demonstrate "backlinking" to Safari.

This would be a link back to Math XL.

The href looks like this:

<a href="https://etext.pearsonxl.com/etextlink.aspx?
            custom_targetId=assignedhomework&
            custom_chapterId=1&
            userparamName=etextuser1&courseParamName=course1" 
            class="lms-link"
            id="12345"
            data-lms="xl-he"
            data-lms-linksdesc="something_to_help_Editorial_identify">Math XL.</a>
